Term of the Day: Reciprocity

Reciprocity refers to the principle in international relations that favors, benefits, or penalties granted by one state to the citizens or legal entities of another should be returned in kind.

It forms a fundamental basis for diplomatic relations, trade agreements, and international law, ensuring fairness and encouraging mutual cooperation or mutual consequences depending on actions.
